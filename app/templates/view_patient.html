PATIALA

Patiala is a city in southeastern Punjab, northwestern India. It is the fourth largest city in the state and is the administrative capital of Patiala district.


Patiala has been consistently performing better than the rest of the districts in punjab in terms of reproductive and child health. The Mother and Child health action plan(2014-2017) is a comprehensive effort by the Government of Punjab to translate the national RMNCH+A strategy for women and children. As a positive indicator, the state’s infant mortality rate (IMR) has witnessed two points dip from last year, reveals Sample Registration Report 2016 released by the central government on September 28. The report states, IMR has dropped from 23 per 1,000 births in 2015 to 21 per 1,000 births in 2016. The same figure was 24 per 1,000 births in 2014 and 26 deaths in 2013.
